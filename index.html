Economic Concepts Behind This Simulator

🏦

RBI Monetary Policy

The RBI repo rate drives MSME borrowing costs. Higher rates → costlier loans → credit constraint. IC = 0.4I × rRBI

📉

Inflation Impact

Rising inflation erodes real profits. Simulator applies 50% pass-through: IE = GP × (π/2), reflecting limited MSME pricing power.

🌱

Green Economics

Sustainability cuts long-run costs (10% saving for high adopters) and attracts ESG credit. Growth bonus g ∈ {0%, 4%, 9%}.

📊

MSME & GDP

MSMEs contribute ~30% to India's GDP and employ 11 crore workers. FHS synthesises profitability, policy, sustainability & inflation resilience.
